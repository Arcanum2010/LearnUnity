This is the GitHub page for the upcoming book, Learn Unity 4 for iOS Game Development, currently available as an Apress alpha book.

The GitHub projects, built up cumulatively through each chapter, do not include assets from the Unity Asset Store and Standard Assets that are used in the book, but the HyperBowl-style bowling game developed by following the book, complete with Asset Store assets and Unity Standard Assets, is on the App Store.

The book and app demonstrate Unity graphics, audio and physics, scripting in JavaScript (and a little bit of C#), touch screen and accelerometer input, iAd banner and interstitial ads, Game Center leaderboard and achievements, complete bowling rules, and utilize free Asset Store packages:

  • Eighteen Free Substances from Allegorithmic
  • Free ArtskillZ Texture Pack 01 from ArtskillZ
  • Skeletons Pack from bisaniyehocam
  • General Music Set from Gianmarco Leone
  • Free SFX Package from Bleep Blop Audio
  • Barrel from Universal Image

The book also covers debugging, profiling and optimization, testing with the Unity Remote and iOS Simulator, build and App Store submission procedures

Questions can be posted on the Unity forum, any of my Facebook pages (Technicat, Fugu Games or HyperBowl) and on twitter.

You may also be interested in Sue Blackman's Beginning 3D Game Development with Unity (also from Apress), Will Goldstone's Unity 3.x Game Development Essentials, and Jeff Murray's Game Development for iOS with Unity3D.
